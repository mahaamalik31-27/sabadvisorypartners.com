The Firm

Independent advice for owners making defining decisions.

SAB Advisory Partners is an independent M&A advisory firm headquartered in the UAE, advising clients globally with a focus on the UAE and UK. We work in the lower mid-market — typically AED 10 million to AED 200 million and above — led by a team with over 30 years of M&A experience across mid-market and large-cap transactions.
