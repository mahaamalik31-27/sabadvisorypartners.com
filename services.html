Services

Advisory support across the full transaction lifecycle.

SAB Advisory Partners advises founders, owners and strategic buyers on business sales, acquisitions, valuation, deal readiness and transaction execution.

Core Services

Focused, senior-led M&A advisory.

01

M&A Advisory

Advising founders, owners and strategic buyers on the sale, acquisition and positioning of lower mid-market businesses. Includes sell-side advisory, buy-side mandates, deal structuring and negotiation support.

02

Transaction Execution

End-to-end management of the process, including buyer outreach, process discipline, diligence coordination, negotiation support and closing coordination.

03

Valuation & Deal Readiness

Independent valuation support, EBITDA normalisation, exit readiness, acquisition readiness, teasers, information memoranda and data-room preparation.

04

Financial Advisory

Pre-transaction clean-up, forecasting, diligence readiness and finance function preparation. Ongoing finance leadership can be delivered through specialist partners.

05

Growth & Strategic Advisory

Growth strategy, market-entry planning, operational efficiency and margin improvement for founders preparing for a transaction or strategic review.

06

Funding Readiness

Business plans, financial models, pitch materials and introduction support for companies preparing for funding conversations.

07

Governance & Structure

Corporate structuring, group governance, holding-company readiness and partner-led legal, audit and tax coordination where required.

Process

A clear path from preparation to completion.

01

Prepare

Understand objectives, review financials, identify value drivers and prepare the business for market or acquisition review.

02

Position

Create a clear narrative, valuation support, teaser, information memorandum and data-room structure.

03

Engage

Identify and engage qualified counterparties with a controlled, confidential and professional process.

04

Execute

Coordinate diligence, negotiation, documentation workflow and the path to completion with senior-level attention.
