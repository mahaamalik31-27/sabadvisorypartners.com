Services

Advisory support across the M&A workstream

SAB Advisory Partners organises the advisory workstreams required for a transaction, delivered through our team or through trusted affiliate partners where specialist services are required.

Primary services

01

M&A Advisory

Advising on business sales, acquisitions, strategic positioning, deal structuring and negotiation.

02

Transaction Execution

Managing the process, counterparty engagement, due-diligence coordination and closing support.

03

Valuation, Due Diligence & Readiness

Valuation support, financial due diligence, EBITDA normalisation, teasers, information memoranda and data-room preparation.

04

Financial Advisory

Pre-transaction clean-up, forecasting, diligence readiness and finance function preparation.

05

Growth & Strategic Advisory

Growth strategy, market-entry planning, operational efficiency and margin improvement.

One-stop shop

Organised through SAB

All advisory workstreams connected to an M&A process can be coordinated through SAB, whether delivered directly by us or by affiliate partners.

Specialist partners

Where needed

For services we cannot provide directly, we coordinate with trusted affiliate partners so clients have one organised process.
